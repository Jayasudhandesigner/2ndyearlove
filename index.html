Our Love in a Poem 💖

The first time our fingers shyly entwined,
Time paused — your hand, so warm in mine.
A silent promise, a quiet start,
Two trembling palms, one beating heart.

Your shoulder brushed, and I felt a sigh,
Soft as petals beneath the sky.
A simple touch, but it held the world,
Where your presence, like starlight, softly swirled.

When you whispered "I love you" — sweet and low,
It bloomed inside me, a gentle glow.
The world faded, it was only you,
And the truth in your eyes — so raw, so true.

Our first video call, nervous and bright,
You smiled through pixels, lit up the night.
Even distance bent to love’s command,
When I couldn’t touch — but still, I held your hand.

You once slept gently on my shoulder's space,
The world grew quiet in that embrace.
Your breath like feathers, slow and deep,
A lullaby I’ll always keep.

We shared a kulfi on a warm noon street,
Your giggle shy, your voice so sweet.
The cold we licked, the warmth we felt,
That quiet joy made my whole heart melt.

When we were close, and you looked away,
Blush blooming like a rose in May.
Your shy smile, your fluttered breath —
Made my every moment feel like death and rebirth.

These memories — they're not just past,
They’re roots of love, forever cast.
And every day, with you, feels new,
Because my forever... began with you.

Made with infinite love 💘
